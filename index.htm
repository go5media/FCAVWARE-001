About FC Aware AV

A volunteer-led football media project focused on grassroots clubs.

What we do

We provide professional-grade football coverage using a multi-camera setup, capturing both wide and close-up angles. Footage is edited into highlights and delivered to clubs after the match.

Why it matters

  • Players can review performance and positioning
  • Coaches gain valuable tactical insight
  • Clubs receive shareable media for promotion and recruitment

Services

Coverage is adapted to suit each club and venue.

Match recording

Multi-camera match capture with edited highlights provided post-match.

Commentary mic setup

Microphone setup available for clubs providing their own commentator.

Livestreaming

Live match broadcast with real-time camera switching.

Requires wired internet and YouTube stream key.

Combined coverage

Livestreaming, recording, and highlights delivered from a single setup.

Coverage area

London-side of the M25 and surrounding South West London areas.

Coverage runs from TW20 (Egham – London side), through KT20 (Tadworth & Walton-on-the-Hill), across South West London to SW11 (Battersea).

Unsure if your ground is within range? Contact us and we’ll confirm.

How it works

Simple, professional, and adaptable.

  1. 1

    Choose your fixture

    Tell us which match you want covered and your preferred setup.

  2. 2

    On-site setup

    We arrive early, set up all equipment, and prepare coverage.

  3. 3

    Match coverage

    Live-operated multi-camera filming throughout the game.

  4. 4

    Delivery

    Highlights and footage delivered as agreed with the club.

Consent & footage handling

Clear, responsible handling of match footage.

  • Club access to footage for up to 2 years
  • Internal quality review retention up to 6 months
  • Deleted within 31 days after final edit unless agreed otherwise

With appropriate consent, footage may be used for promotional purposes. Individuals without consent may be obscured in edited videos where possible.
