How it works

Simple, predictable, and designed to stay out of the way.

Before kick-off

Equipment is brought in and installed before the match begins. Cables are routed safely. Cameras are positioned once and not physically moved during play.

During the match

The PTZ camera is operated remotely from a single position using a controller. The wide camera provides full-pitch context.

After full-time

Equipment is packed down and removed. Footage is edited into highlights and shared with the club after the match.

What the club provides: Reliable power + a small table/space near the touchline. • For livestreams: Wired internet + YouTube stream key.

Coverage area

South West London and nearby parts of Surrey (within and around the M25). Roughly between Tadworth and Battersea.

Typical areas

  • Carshalton, Wallington, Sutton, Mitcham
  • Chessington, Worcester Park, New Malden
  • Tadworth and nearby M25 edge areas
  • South West London through to Battersea

Not sure if a ground is covered? Send a message — it’s often workable if it’s within the corridor.

What’s not covered

  • Locations well beyond the M25 (e.g., deep Surrey countryside)
  • One-off long-distance requests outside the corridor

The aim is reliable local coverage, not occasional far travel.

Safeguarding and conduct

Designed to be predictable, low-interaction, and match-focused.

Match day conduct

  • Equipment installed before kick-off and removed after full-time
  • Operation from a single fixed position
  • No entry onto the pitch during play
  • Any necessary interactions are brief and in public

Footage handling

  • Footage stored privately for clubs for up to 2 years
  • Internal review retained up to 6 months
  • Raw footage deleted within 31 days after final edit unless agreed otherwise
  • Clubs can request public/unlisted/private livestream settings

Promotional use may occur unless the club requests otherwise. For livestreams, real-time blurring is not possible.

Quick FAQ

Is it really free?

Yes. FC AV Ware is volunteer-run. Donations are optional and never required.

What does the club need to provide?

Reliable power and a small table/space. For livestreams: a wired internet connection and stream key.

Will filming disrupt the match?

No. Cameras are installed before kick-off and operated from one position. No cameras are moved during play.
